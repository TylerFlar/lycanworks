Terms of Service

Will & Won't Create

I Will Create

  • Original characters (OCs)
  • Fanart
  • Anthro / Furry
  • Feral characters
  • Mild gore / blood
  • Couples / multiple characters (additional charges apply)
  • NSFW (at my discretion, additional charges apply)

I Won't Create

  • Hateful or discriminatory content
  • Real people without their consent
  • Anything illegal
  • Complex mechanical designs (mecha, vehicles, etc.)

If unsure, just ask!

Payment
  • Work on the commission will only begin when full payment is received in $USD. For commissions exceeding $500, a 50% before/50% after agreement may be made at my discretion—this must be mutually agreed upon before payment.
  • Tips are appreciated but do not alter the service provided nor my obligation to you.
Process & Delivery
  • Work-in-progress (WIP) images will be sent at various stages. You may request reasonable changes—what's 'reasonable' depends on the commission scope and work completed. Major changes may incur additional charges at my discretion.
  • After sending a WIP, I am not obligated to wait for a response before continuing.
  • An approximate delivery time will be provided, but this is not a binding deadline. You'll be notified of significant delays. Delays will not exceed 60 days—if exceeded, you're entitled to a full refund.
  • Upon completion you'll receive: full resolution PNG, a web-friendly copy (under 10MB), and a square headshot for profile use. No physical product, but you may print for personal use.

Alterations

  • I will execute the commission to the best of my ability. If you provide references, I may make minor artistic alterations to improve quality. Major design changes will be discussed first.
  • Edit requests after final delivery will likely be charged unless trivial (under 5 minutes).
  • I am under no obligation to provide further alterations or service after the final product is delivered.
