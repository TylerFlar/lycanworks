Terms of Service

Will & Won't Create

Will Create

  • Original characters (OCs) and fanart
  • Anthro, furry, and feral characters
  • Mild gore / blood
  • Multiple characters (additional charges apply)
  • NSFW at my discretion (additional charges apply)

Won't Create

  • Hateful, discriminatory, or illegal content
  • NSFW involving minors in any context
  • Real people without their explicit consent
  • Complex mechanical designs (mecha, vehicles, etc.)

If unsure about your idea, just ask — I don't bite.

Payment & Pricing
  • Full payment in USD before work begins. For commissions over $500, a 50/50 split may be arranged at my discretion.
  • Your quoted price is locked in at time of agreement, regardless of future price changes on the site.
  • Scope changes after payment (adding characters, complexity, etc.) may incur additional charges — these will be discussed before proceeding.
  • Tips are appreciated but do not alter the scope of service.
Process & Delivery
  • Provide clear references — the more detail, the better the result.
  • WIPs shared at key stages. 2 revision rounds per WIP included; major changes (new pose, redesign) may cost extra.
  • If I don't hear back within 48 hours of a WIP, I may continue working.
  • Delivery time is approximate. Delays over 60 days entitle you to a full refund.
  • You'll receive: full-res PNG, web-friendly copy, and a square headshot crop.
  • Post-delivery edits are charged unless trivial (under 5 min).

Communication

  • I respond within a few days. Please keep discussion in one thread/DM.
  • If you go unresponsive for 30 days, I may cancel the commission. Refund depends on work completed.
Copyright, Usage & AI

The Artist (Me)

  • I retain copyright over the artwork but don't claim your characters or IP
  • I may post the artwork for portfolio/marketing purposes
  • You may share, display, and print the artwork for personal use
  • You may not profit from, alter, or claim the artwork as your own
  • My signature/credit must remain visible (cropping is fine)

AI

  • My artwork may not be used for AI training, LORAs, or similar models
  • AI-generated references are fine for communicating ideas — the final piece is 100% hand-drawn
Cancellations & Refunds
  • You may cancel before work begins for a full refund.
  • Once work has started, refunds are at my discretion and may be partial based on work completed.
  • If I am unable to complete your commission, you will receive a full refund.
  • Refunds will be issued directly — do not file a PayPal chargeback. Filing a chargeback forfeits all your rights to the artwork and IP, and the dispute will be contested.
